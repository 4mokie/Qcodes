qcodes.halt_bg¶

qcodes.halt_bg(timeout=5, traceback=True)[source]¶

Stop the active background measurement process, if any.

Parameters:
  • timeout (int) – seconds to wait for a clean exit before forcibly terminating.
  • traceback (bool) – whether to print a traceback at the point of interrupt, for debugging purposes.
